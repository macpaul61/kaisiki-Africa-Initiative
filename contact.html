Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Just send us a message above.

How do I donate to Kaisiki? ▾
Visit our Get Involved page to donate via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or an international payment gateway. You can also email us directly and we'll guide you through the process.
Where does my donation go? ▾
100% of donations go to program delivery — school fees, supplies, mentorship, and community outreach in Tanzania. We publish annual financial reports for full transparency.
Can I volunteer if I'm not in Tanzania? ▾
Yes. We have remote volunteer roles in grant writing, communications, social media, web support, and translation. Apply via the Get Involved page.
How can my organisation partner with you? ▾
We welcome partnerships with schools, corporates, foundations, and NGOs. Contact us at partners@kaisikiafrica.org or use the form above — select "Partnership / Sponsorship" as the enquiry type.
How quickly will you respond to my message? ▾
We aim to respond to all messages within 2 business days. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us.
Is Kaisiki a registered NGO? ▾
Yes. Kaisiki African Initiative is a registered non-governmental organisation in Tanzania. Registration details are available on request for due diligence purposes.
