Education Access Program

We believe education is the foundation for lasting change. Our Education Access Program removes barriers to quality education for children and youth across Africa — from primary school through tertiary level.

  • Scholarships for underprivileged students
  • School supplies and uniform distribution
  • After-school tutoring and mentorship
  • Infrastructure improvements for rural schools
  • Digital literacy and technology access

Health & Wellness

Improving community health outcomes through education, expanded access to services, and preventive care initiatives — with a special focus on maternal and child health in underserved areas.

  • Community health worker training

Women & Girls Empowerment
