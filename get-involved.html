Your Money at Work

Here's exactly what different amounts achieve for a girl in Tanzania:

📓
$10 / Tsh 26,000 Covers one month of exercise books, pens, and basic stationery for a student.
👗
$25 / Tsh 65,000 Provides a full school uniform so a girl can attend class without fear of being sent home.
📚
$50 / Tsh 130,000 Funds one term's worth of textbooks across all core subjects for a secondary student.
🏫
$150 / Tsh 390,000 Covers one full term of school fees — the single biggest barrier to education for most families.
🎓
$500 / Tsh 1,300,000 Sponsors a girl's entire school year — fees, books, uniform, and mentorship sessions.

Give Your Time & Skills

We don't need you to be an expert in development work. We need teachers, designers, marketers, accountants, counsellors, photographers, and people who care.

Partner With Us

We welcome partnerships with organisations, foundations, schools, and businesses that share our belief in girls' education and community development.

🏫

School & Education Partners

Partner schools receive support, resources, and a direct link to our scholarship and mentorship programs for their students.

🏢

Corporate CSR Partners

Tanzanian and international companies can align their CSR work with our programs — sponsoring a school term, providing equipment, or funding specific initiatives.

🌍

International Foundations

We are open to co-funding arrangements with foundations and NGOs working in East Africa's education and gender equity space.

🎓

Universities & Research

Academic institutions can partner with us for research, student placements, or curriculum support that benefits our program participants.

Interested in discussing a partnership? Our team is happy to explore what collaboration could look like for your organisation.
